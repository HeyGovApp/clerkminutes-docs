---
title: "Departments"
description: "Learn how departments & members are integrated in ClerkMinutes"
---

The real-life departments in a municipality are integrated seamlessly the meeting workflow:

- all department types are supported: departments, boards, committees, commissions
- meetings are linked to departments, ex: "Board Meeting" belongs to "Town Board"
- members in departments have different permissions for a meeting: the department that organizes the meeting has full access, other members collaborate or be assigned as speakers
- each department can have different templates for agenda & minutes documents
