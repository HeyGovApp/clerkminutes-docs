### Overview

The Agendas are an important resource in creating the minutes. It tells the AI what to focus on while listening through the recording.

Agendas can be [created](/agendas/build) or imported in ClerkMinutes. If you have an agenda prepared outside ClerkMinutes, here's how to import it (agenda items, descriptions, speakers, and more):

### Steps to import an agenda

1. Head over to the meeting page, then open the `Agenda` tab
2. Localte the "Import existing agenda" area
3. Click on the button to browse for a file, or drag&drop the file in the area
4. Allow ClerkMinutes up to 2 minutes to upload & process the agenda
5. Review the imported content

### Supported agenda documents

Currently ClerkMinutes supports importing documents with a maxmium file size of **30mb** and following formats:

- PDF (`.pdf`)
- Word Document (`.doc`, `.docx`)
